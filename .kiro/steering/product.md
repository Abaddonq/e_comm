# Product Overview

This is a production-ready Laravel e-commerce platform designed for selling decorative physical products (0-1000 items). The system handles the complete order lifecycle from product browsing through payment processing to shipment tracking.

## Target Environment

- Deployment: Shared hosting (Hostinger Business Plan)
- Scale: Small to medium traffic, up to 1000 products
- Infrastructure: Standard PHP + MySQL (no Docker, Redis, or Node services)

## Core Capabilities

- Product catalog with categories, variants, and images
- Shopping cart for authenticated and guest users
- Secure checkout with payment gateway integration (iyzico/Stripe)
- Order management with automated stock tracking
- Shipment tracking and notifications
- Admin panel for product, order, and inventory management
- SEO optimization with slugs, meta tags, and sitemap
- Email notifications for key events

## Business Model

The platform targets commercial decorative product sales with features for:
- Multiple product variants (size, color, material)
- Real-time inventory tracking via stock movements
- Flexible shipping cost calculation
- Order lifecycle management from payment to delivery
